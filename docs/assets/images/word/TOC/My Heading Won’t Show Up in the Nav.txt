My Heading Won’t Show Up in the Navigation Pane”
Problem: You apply a heading style, but it doesn’t appear in the left-hand outline. Fix:

Confirm it’s a built-in Heading 1/2/3 style — not a custom style with similar formatting.

Check that the paragraph isn’t inside a text box or table (Word ignores those in the Nav Pane).
