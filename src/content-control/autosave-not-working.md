<h4>I don't see the Autosave toggle</h4>
<p>The Autosave toggle only appears in apps if you have Microsoft Office 365 subscription. If you have a standalone one-time subscription like Microsoft Office 2021 or a single app like Word 2019 this isn't available.</p>
<p>If you're using Office apps on your mobile or the free online version like Word online then your work is autosaved as you edit so you don't need a toggle.</p>
<br>

<h4>Autosave isn't working for my file</h4>
<p>Autosave has to save to either OneDrive or SharePoint, these are cloud-based apps meaning they're stored online. So you can't use them if you're only saving locally (on your computer).</p>

<p>For Autosave to work the filepath you save to should look like this:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>OneDrive</strong>: C:\Users\<u>YourUserID</u>\OneDrive\Presentation.pptx</li>
  <li><strong>SharePoint</strong>: https://<u>yourcompany</u>.sharepoint.com/sites/<u>TeamName</u>/Shared Documents/Presentation.pptx
</li>
</ul>

<p>If you're saving locally (i.e. to your computer) with a filepath like this Autosave won't work:</p>
<ul>
  <li>C:\Users\<u>YourUserID</u>\Desktop</li>
</ul>
